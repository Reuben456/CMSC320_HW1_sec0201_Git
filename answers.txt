Q1: Why might we consider using Git?
Answer: Git helps us track different versions and allows us to collaborate on projects, and have more flexibility in terms of keeping drafts and the final product

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an example.
Answer: Agriculture - tracking how plants grow over large datasets could be helpful in improving crop yield
Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?
Answer: Merge combines the past files and combines them all into a commit. Rebase is important in adding the commits to the specific branch to add to the history
